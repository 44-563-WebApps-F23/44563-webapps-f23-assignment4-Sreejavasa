REGINA.

I won't have any one find you here; so just you go about your business.

ENGSTRAND.

[Advances a step or two.]

Blest if I go before I've had a talk with you.

This afternoon I shall have finished my work at the school house, and then I shall take to-night's boat and be off home to the town.

REGINA.

[Mutters.]

Pleasant journey to you!
